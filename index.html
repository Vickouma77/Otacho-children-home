About Us

Welcome to Otacho Christian Children's Home, a beacon of hope in the heart of Migori, Kenya. Based on the principles of love, faith and compassion, our home provides a caring environment for 142 children in need, instills Christian values ​​and ensures the holistic development of children. We strive to do so. Our commitment extends beyond the basic needs of food, shelter, and clothing. At Otacho Christian Children's Home, we believe in empowering the next generation through education. We strive to ensure that each child in our care has access to quality education, equipping them with the knowledge and skills necessary to break the cycle of poverty and reach their full potential The Otacho Christian Children's Home is not just a shelter; it is a sanctuary where dreams are nurtured, and futures are shaped. We envision a world where every child, regardless of their circumstances, has the opportunity to thrive and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our History

Founded in 2003 by Nashon Otiato, Our story is one of compassion, rooted in the personal experience of our founder, Nashon Otiato. At the tender age of 8, Nashon found himself orphaned and facing an uncertain future. However, a benevolent Samaritan intervened, providing Nashon with more than just shelter and sustenance. This act of kindness bestowed upon him the gift of education, nurturing his potential and laying the foundation for the philanthropist he would become. vDriven by the profound impact of generosity, Nashon, along with his wife Judith Ouma, established Otacho Christian Children's Home with a mission to pay forward the kindness that changed his life. Together, they have opened their hearts and home to 142 orphans, raising them as if they were their own. The couple, along with their three biological children, form a tight-knit family that is committed to providing love, care, and a supportive environment for every child under their roof.

Organization's Strategic Objectives

Mission

Our mission as Otacho Children's Home:

Our mission at Otacho Children's Home is to provide a nurturing haven for children in need. We strive to holistically foster their growth—physically, emotionally, socially, and intellectually. Beyond immediate care, we empower them with life skills and quality education, preparing them for a future of hope and purpose.

Vision

Vision for the home:

Our vision is to create a community where every child experiences love, support, and the opportunity to reach their full potential. At Otacho, we aspire to build a foundation for positive change, fostering an environment where children thrive and contribute positively to society.

Goals

our goals:

Our goals encompass providing a safe and loving environment, ensuring access to quality education, and instilling essential life skills. We aim to empower children to overcome challenges and become confident, responsible individuals who make meaningful contributions to their communities.

Holistic Approach

Emotional Support

Foster a caring environment where children at Otacho feel secure and loved. We provide opportunities for them to express emotions, build positive relationships, and offer counseling to help cope with any experienced trauma.

Spiritual Growth

We nurture the spiritual development of our children, teaching them about God's love and providing chances to practice their faith. We encourage the formation of their personal beliefs and values.

Educational Empowerment

Offering a comprehensive academic education, we ensure our children meet national curriculum standards. Our dedicated educators create engaging learning environments tailored to individual needs, aiming to instill a love for learning and curiosity.

Future Planning

We envision a future where each child realizes their potential. Plans include expanding educational programs, fostering career development, and strengthening health services. Our commitment extends to family reunification, transitional living initiatives, and sustainable practices.

Life Skill Training

Acknowledging the importance of preparing children for independent lives, our life skills training focuses on practical abilities essential for adulthood. From self-care to financial literacy, we tailor a comprehensive curriculum to instill confidence and competence.

Social Integration

Social integration is fundamental at Otacho Christian Children's Home. We prioritize creating an inclusive environment where children develop strong interpersonal skills and a sense of community. Through collaborative activities and cultural celebrations, we foster a deep sense of belonging and social connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do you ensure a loving environment for the children?

We prioritize creating a nurturing atmosphere where children feel secure and loved. Our dedicated team ensures emotional support and provides counseling to help them cope with any challenges they may face.

Do you offer youth programs for older children?

Absolutely! We believe in empowering older children through various youth programs. These programs include life skills training, educational support, and career development to prepare them for a successful future.

How can I support family reunification efforts?

Supporting family reunification is crucial. You can contribute by participating in our mentorship programs, providing resources, and helping create a supportive community for families to reunite successfully.
